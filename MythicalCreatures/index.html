The Mighty Dragon

Dragons are legendary creatures found in folklore worldwide, often depicted as powerful, fire-breathing reptiles. They originated in 6200 BC in both Chinese and Mesopotamian culture. They have always been revered and feared. They are often depicted in modern culture surrounded by mountains of gold and precious things.

The Immortal Phoenix

The Phoenix is a legendary bird known for its cycle of death and rebirth. Found in ancient Egyptian, Greek, and Chinese mythology, the Phoenix is said to burst into flames upon death, only to rise from its own ashes, renewed and stronger. This magnificent creature symbolizes immortality, resilience, and the eternal cycle of life.

The Majestic Griffin

The Griffin, a majestic hybrid of an eagle and a lion, is a creature of wisdom and strength. Originating in ancient Mesopotamian and Greek mythology, it is often depicted as a guardian of treasure and a symbol of divine power. With the keen eyesight of an eagle and the courage of a lion, the Griffin is revered as a noble protector of kings and sacred places.
